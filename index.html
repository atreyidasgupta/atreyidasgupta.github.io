INTRODUCTION

Hi! I'm Atreyi, a recent MSc Physics graduate from BITS Pilani, Hyderabad. My background is in astronomy and high-energy astrophysics — I've spent the last few years building automated pipelines, running statistical analyses, and extracting meaningful signals from large, noisy datasets in various projects in BITS Pilani Hyderabad and IIT Bombay. I'm now channeling that same quantitative rigour into business and data problems. I'm drawn to work that sits at the intersection of structured thinking and real-world impact — whether that's uncovering patterns in data, translating complexity into clear decisions, or figuring out why things work the way they do.

BACKGROUND

I was born in Delhi, but I have spent the majority of my life in Mumbai. I completed my schooling in Navi Mumbai, where I developed my passion for dancing, singing, and poetry writing, achieving recognition and winning prizes in all three disciplines at various stages of my education. Following high school, I relocated to Hyderabad to pursue my Integrated Master's degree.
